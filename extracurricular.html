      Mathematics Club

      AIM: The Mathematics Club aims to work for the interests of our fellow students. We develop students’ mathematical curiosity and interpersonal relationship skills by conducting member meetings with various topics related to Mathematics. The purpose of the Mathematics Club is to arouse students’ interests in Mathematics so that they can become confident in using mathematics to analyze and solve problems both in school and in real-life situations. Mathematics is a tool to train one’s logical thinking skills. It is also a sword which can solve thousands of problems in various fields in our daily life.

      Music Association Choir

      AIM: To bond students together with enthusiasm and a shared love and passion for music.

      OBJECTIVES: 1. Let the students to achieve higher standard musical performances 2. Let the students to increase their interest in music. 3. Strengthen the students on communication and the ability of planning and organising activities. The School Choir, the School Orchestra, the Percussion Band, and the Handbell Team are all teams under the supervision of the Music Association. All four teams have regular practices and internal meetings among officers. Besides, several interest classes including the newly established Wind band, percussion interest classes, and cello ensemble are under the management of the Music Association as well. The Music Association aims to bond students together with enthusiasm and a shared love and passion for music. With the common goal to achieve high standard musical performances, the four teams have participated in various musical events of every scholastic year like Music Festival, Lunch Concert, Christmas Flash Performance, Christmas Party Performance etc. Students have also taking part in various external events and competitions such as the Inter-School Singing Competition and the MYSO concerts.
